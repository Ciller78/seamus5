About Me

Who am I?

I’m a lovable little mischief-maker — a proud mix of Yorkshire Terrier and Bichon Frise with eyes so human-like, I’ve been told I could win a staring contest with anyone!

Where I’m From

I split my time between home and the gorgeous beaches of Nefyn, North Wales, where I love to chase birds, dig in the sand, and hang out with my doggy cousins, Magic and Merlin. Merlin is my partner-in-crime, while Magic tends to stick close to the humans and keep us out of too much trouble.

What I Love

  • Zoomies on the beach
  • Sneaking Snack when nobody is looking
  • Long naps in cozy spots
  • Making new friends - dogs and humans alike
  • Car rides, especially if we end up in Nefyn
